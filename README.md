# 🕸️ Text to Graph Organizer 🕸️

Welcome to **Text to Graph Organizer**! 🚀 A clever application that takes your text and intelligently organizes the hidden information into a beautiful, hierarchical relational graph. 🧠✨

## 🌟 Features

- **Multi-Format Support**: Whether it's a web URL 🌐 or a local file 📄 (`.txt`, `.pdf`, `.docx`, `.odt`), we can extract the text!
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Under the hood, we use a local **Ollama** LLM 🦙 to deeply analyze your content and extract the core meaning and sub-themes.
- **Relational Summarization**: We don't just summarize; we map out the connections! The main theme acts as the **source node** 🎯, connecting downward to first-order themes 🌿, and continuing down to lower-level concepts. 
- **Interactive Visualization**: See your data come to life! Cycles are permitted, and our interactive graph (built with Streamlit-agraph) automatically organizes itself so you can explore complex concepts visually. 🎨

## 🚀 Getting Started

Thanks to our sleek **Streamlit interface** 🖥️, extracting knowledge is as easy as 1-2-3:
1. **Fire up the app** and configure your preferred local Ollama model in the sidebar ⚙️.
2. **Provide your content** by either dragging and dropping a document, or directly pasting a URL 🖇️.
3. **Generate the graph** and watch as your block of text magically transforms into a visual web of interconnected thoughts! 🕸️
